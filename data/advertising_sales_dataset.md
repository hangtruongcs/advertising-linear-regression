# Advertising Sales Dataset

## Description

The Advertising Sales dataset contains sales revenue alongside advertising budgets across three media channels: TV, Radio, and Newspaper. It is used to practice linear regression and understand how ad spend influences sales.

## Columns

- `TV Ad Budget ($)` — budget spent on TV ads
- `Radio Ad Budget ($)` — budget spent on radio ads
- `Newspaper Ad Budget ($)` — budget spent on newspaper ads
- `Sales ($)` — total sales revenue *(target)*

## Source

- **Platform:** Kaggle
- **Link:** [Advertising Sales Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/yasserh/advertising-sales-dataset/data)
